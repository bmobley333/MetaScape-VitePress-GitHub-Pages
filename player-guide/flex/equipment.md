# Weapons & Gear (Flex Engine)

In the Flex engine, equipment provides concrete mechanical bonuses, armor ratings, and block limits. While anyone can attempt to use any weapon or armor, you roll with **Disadvantage** if you do not meet the item's attribute requirements.
