# Using the Flex Web App

The **MetaScape Flex Web App** is a digital character sheet and dice roller companion. It allows players to manage characters and roll abilities with single-click ease.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **One-Click Rolling**: Select your governing attribute die, enter bonuses, and roll immediately.
* **Automatic Roll Curves**: The app automatically handles **Skilled** (2H20), **Unskilled** (1d20), **Advantage** (3H20), and **Disadvantage** (2L20) selections.
* **Vitality & Luck Tracking**: Interactive heart and clover trackers let you log wounds and spend Luck chits.
* **Powers Database**: Checkboxes for per-encounter and per-day usages reset automatically when initiating short or long rests.

---

## 📝 Setting Up Your Character

1. **Create Character**: Open the app, click **New Character**, and fill in your concept, traits, appearance, and goals.
2. **Set Attributes**: Input your starting die sizes (d4, d4, d6, d8) for Might💪, Motion🏃, Mind👁️, and Magic✨.
3. **Equip Items**: Select your weapons, armor, and shields from the drop-down equipment catalog. The app automatically calculates your:
   - **Base Dodge** (Motion)
   - **Base Block** (Might)
   - **Movement Rate (MR)** (reduced automatically by armor penalties)
4. **Choose Powers**: Add your 3 starting powers⚡ to the tracker.

---

## 🎲 Rolling from the Dashboard

To make an ability check:
1. Click the attribute name (e.g., **Might💪**).
2. The roll dialog will pop up showing your base die size (e.g. `d8`).
3. Select your proficiency state:
   - **Skilled** (Roll 2H20 keep highest)
   - **Unskilled** (Roll 1d20)
4. Set situational options like **Advantage** (adds +1 d20) or **Disadvantage** (removes 1 d20).
5. Enter flat bonuses (e.g., weapon bonus +1, traits +1).
6. Click **ROLL** to push the result and GM narrative tags (Tremendous/Critical check) to the chat log.
